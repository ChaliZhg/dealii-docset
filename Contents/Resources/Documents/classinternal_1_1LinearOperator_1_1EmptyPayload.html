Detailed Description

A dummy class for LinearOperators that do not require any extensions to facilitate the operations of the matrix.

This is the Payload class typically associated with deal.II's native SparseMatrix. To use Trilinos and PETSc SparseMatrices it is necessary to initialize a LinearOperator with their associated Payload.

Author
Jean-Paul Pelteret, Matthias Maier, 2016
