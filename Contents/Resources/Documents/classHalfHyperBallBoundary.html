Detailed Description

template<int dim>
class HalfHyperBallBoundary< dim >

Variant of HyperBallBoundary which denotes a half hyper ball where the first coordinate is restricted to the range (or ). In two dimensions, this equals the right half circle, in three space dimensions it is a half ball. This class might be useful for computations with rotational symmetry, where one dimension is the radius from the axis of rotation.

Author
Wolfgang Bangerth, 1999, 2001
